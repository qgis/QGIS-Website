{{< columns-start >}}

### The Premier Open Source Geospatial Conference

**Auckland, New Zealand | November 17-23, 2025**

FOSS4G (Free and Open Source Software for Geospatial) is the annual global event for the open source geospatial community. Since 2004, FOSS4G has brought together thousands of developers, users, decision-makers, and observers from around the world.

**What to Expect:**

- Workshops and training sessions
- Technical presentations and talks
- Code sprints
- Business track for enterprise users
- Academic track for researchers
- Social events and networking
- Exhibition of sponsors and projects

**Conference Features:**

- 1,000+ participants from 60+ countries
- 200+ presentations across multiple tracks
- 50+ workshops from beginner to advanced
- Opportunities to meet project maintainers
- Travel grants available for eligible participants

<h2 class="section-title">Host an Event</h2>
</div>

Interested in hosting a FOSS4G or regional event? OSGeo supports local organizers with:

{{< columns-start >}}

{{< column-start class="is-8" >}}

- **Branding and recognition** as an official OSGeo event
- **Access to community** mailing lists for promotion
- **Experience and guidance** from previous organizers
- **Connection to sponsors** interested in supporting events
- **Infrastructure support** for registration and logistics
