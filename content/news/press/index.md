## Media Resources

### About OSGeo

The Open Source Geospatial Foundation (OSGeo) is a not-for-profit organization founded in 2006 to support and promote the collaborative development of open source geospatial software. OSGeo provides financial, organizational, and legal support to over 50 projects used by millions worldwide.

### Key Facts

- **Founded:** 2006
- **Projects:** 50+ open source geospatial projects
- **Community:** 300+ Charter Members from 60+ countries
- **Events:** FOSS4G annual conference since 2006
- **Reach:** Millions of users worldwide
