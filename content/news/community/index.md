#### New Charter Members Elected

*2024*

We are pleased to welcome our newest Charter Members who have demonstrated outstanding contributions to the open source geospatial community.

Charter Members are elected by existing members in recognition of sustained contributions to OSGeo projects, documentation, advocacy, and community building.

<h2 class="section-title">Get Involved</h2>
</div>

The OSGeo community is always welcoming new contributors. There are many ways to participate:

- **Code** - Contribute to any OSGeo project
- **Documentation** - Help improve user and developer guides
- **Translation** - Make software accessible in more languages
- **Events** - Organize or help at local meetups
- **Support** - Answer questions on mailing lists

[Get Involved](/community/get-involved/){.button .is-osgeo-secondary .is-medium}
