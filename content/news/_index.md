### FOSS4G 2025 Registration Now Open

*November 2024*

FOSS4G 2025 is coming to Auckland, New Zealand! The premier global conference for open source geospatial technology will be held November 17-23, 2025. Early bird registration is now available.

[Learn More About FOSS4G 2025 &rarr;](/initiatives/foss4g/)

---

### QGIS 3.40 LTR Released

*October 2024*

The latest Long Term Release of QGIS is now available, featuring improved performance, new processing algorithms, and enhanced 3D visualization capabilities.

[Download QGIS](https://qgis.org/download/)

---

### OSGeo Welcomes New Charter Members

*September 2024*

We are pleased to announce the election of new charter members who have demonstrated outstanding contributions to the open source geospatial community.

[View All Charter Members &rarr;](/membership/charter-members/)
