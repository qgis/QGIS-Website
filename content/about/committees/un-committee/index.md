The UN Committee aims to contribute to the member states and UN committees by integrating the OSGeo Service Providers, the GeoForAll Academic Network and the OSGeo community.
{{< box-end >}}

<div class="section-header">
<div class="section-header-badge">
<i class="fa-solid fa-clock-rotate-left"></i>
<span>Background</span>
</div>
<h2 class="section-title">Historical Background</h2>
</div>

The committee originated from a 2015 meeting between the OSGeo Board and the UN GIS team at FOSS4G in Seoul, Korea. Kyoung-Soo Eom, head of the UN Geospatial Information Section, invited OSGeo to participate in a working group on Geospatial Information and Disaster Services.

### Service Provider Networks

Provide UN member states with networks of service providers for OSGeo projects.

{{< rich-box-start icon="fa-solid fa-graduation-cap" >}}
### Academic Access

Make the GeoForAll Academic network accessible to UN initiatives.
{{< rich-box-end >}}

{{< rich-box-start icon="fa-solid fa-calendar-days" >}}
### Event Demonstrations

Demonstrate benefits of stable OSGeo project stacks through events.
{{< rich-box-end >}}

{{< column-start class="is-6" >}}

{{< rich-box-start icon="fa-solid fa-comments" >}}
### Communication

Establish communication between UN-Open GIS teams and the OSGeo community.
{{< rich-box-end >}}

{{< rich-box-start icon="fa-solid fa-bullhorn" >}}
### Outreach

Promote OSGeo outreach activities to UN stakeholders.
