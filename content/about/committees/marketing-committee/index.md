The Marketing Committee is OSGeo's official committee dedicated to user outreach and community growth. The committee supports events, projects, and local chapters in marketing efforts.
{{< box-end >}}

<div class="section-header">
<div class="section-header-badge">
<i class="fa-solid fa-bullseye"></i>
<span>Mandate</span>
</div>
<h2 class="section-title">Committee Mandate</h2>
</div>

The committee focuses on executing a medium term marketing strategy geared at growing our community with new users through:

- Ongoing event support with downloadable marketing materials
- Website and logo design updates
- Marketing collateral development and refinement
