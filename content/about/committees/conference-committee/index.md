<h2 class="section-title">Key Responsibilities</h2>
</div>

The Conference Committee oversees:

{{< columns-start >}}

{{< column-start class="is-6" >}}

- Facilitating hosting application and decision processes
- Assisting local conference organizing committees
- Ensuring OSGeo foundation principles are maintained

{{< column-end >}}

{{< column-start class="is-6" >}}

- Supporting affiliated conferences
- Managing travel grant programs for FOSS4G and other OSGeo events

{{< column-end >}}
