<h2 class="section-title">What is Incubation?</h2>
</div>

OSGeo's incubation process is the pathway for open source geospatial projects to become officially recognized as OSGeo projects. The process ensures that projects meet community standards for openness, quality, and sustainability.

<div class="section-header-badge">

<h2 class="section-title">Incubation Criteria</h2>
</div>

<p class="is-size-5 mb-5">Projects are evaluated on the following criteria:</p>

<div class="content-panel-grid">

<div class="content-panel is-featured">
<div class="content-panel-header">
<div class="content-panel-icon">
<i class="fa-solid fa-scale-balanced"></i>
</div>
<div>
<h4 class="content-panel-title">Open Source License</h4>
<p class="content-panel-subtitle">Legal Requirements</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="content-panel-body">
<ul class="checklist">
<li>Must use an OSI-approved open source license</li>
<li>All dependencies must be compatible</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

<div class="content-panel">
<div class="content-panel-header">
<div class="content-panel-icon">
<i class="fa-solid fa-users"></i>
</div>
<div>
<h4 class="content-panel-title">Community</h4>
<p class="content-panel-subtitle">Social Requirements</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="content-panel-body">
<ul class="checklist">
<li>Open and welcoming community</li>
<li>Clear contribution guidelines</li>
<li>Responsive to bug reports and questions</li>
<li>Multiple active contributors</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

<div class="content-panel is-cyan">
<div class="content-panel-header">
<div class="content-panel-icon">
<i class="fa-solid fa-book"></i>
</div>
<div>
<h4 class="content-panel-title">Documentation</h4>
<p class="content-panel-subtitle">Knowledge Sharing</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="content-panel-body">
<ul class="checklist">
<li>User documentation</li>
<li>Developer documentation</li>
<li>Installation guides</li>
<li>API documentation (if applicable)</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

<div class="content-panel is-teal">
<div class="content-panel-header">
<div class="content-panel-icon">
<i class="fa-solid fa-code-branch"></i>
</div>
<div>
<h4 class="content-panel-title">Code Quality</h4>
<p class="content-panel-subtitle">Technical Standards</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="content-panel-body">
<ul class="checklist">
<li>Version control (typically Git)</li>
<li>Issue tracking</li>
<li>Quality assurance practices</li>
<li>Regular releases</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

<div class="content-panel is-amber">
<div class="content-panel-header">
<div class="content-panel-icon">
<i class="fa-solid fa-landmark"></i>
</div>
<div>
<h4 class="content-panel-title">Governance</h4>
<p class="content-panel-subtitle">Leadership Structure</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="content-panel-body">
<ul class="checklist">
<li>Clear decision-making process</li>
<li>Project steering committee or equivalent</li>
<li>Transparent communication</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

</div>

<h2 class="section-title">The Incubation Process</h2>
</div>

<div class="timeline">
<div class="timeline-item">
<div class="timeline-marker is-success"></div>
<div class="timeline-content">
<p class="heading">Step 1</p>
<h4>Application</h4>
<p>Project submits an application to the Incubation Committee</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="timeline-item">
<div class="timeline-marker is-success"></div>
<div class="timeline-content">
<p class="heading">Step 2</p>
<h4>Review</h4>
<p>Committee reviews the application and may request additional information</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="timeline-item">
<div class="timeline-marker is-success"></div>
<div class="timeline-content">
<p class="heading">Step 3</p>
<h4>Mentor Assignment</h4>
<p>If accepted into incubation, a mentor is assigned to guide the project</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="timeline-item">
<div class="timeline-marker is-success"></div>
<div class="timeline-content">
<p class="heading">Step 4</p>
<h4>Incubation Period</h4>
<p>Project works to meet all graduation criteria with mentor support</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="timeline-item">
<div class="timeline-marker is-success"></div>
<div class="timeline-content">
<p class="heading">Step 5</p>
<h4>Graduation</h4>
<p>Committee reviews and recommends graduation to the Board</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
