<h2 class="section-title">Start a Chapter</h2>
</div>

Interested in starting a local chapter in your region? OSGeo welcomes new chapters and provides guidance.

{{< columns-start >}}

{{< column-start class="is-8" >}}

### Requirements

- A group of committed volunteers (minimum 3-5 people)
- Regular planned activities (meetups, workshops, events)
- Alignment with OSGeo's mission and Code of Conduct
- Willingness to report activities to OSGeo

### Benefits

- Use of OSGeo name and branding
- Support from OSGeo board and existing chapters
- Infrastructure support (mailing lists, wiki space)
- Connection to the global OSGeo community

{{< column-end >}}

{{< column-start class="is-4" >}}
{{< rich-box-start >}}
### Get Started

Contact OSGeo to discuss starting a local chapter in your region.

[Contact OSGeo &rarr;](/about/contact/)
