## OSGeo Mailing Lists

Mailing lists are the primary way the OSGeo community communicates. They're used for:

- **Asking questions** and getting help
- **Discussing development** and new features
- **Announcing releases** and important news
- **Coordinating contributions** and activities

## General OSGeo Lists

These lists cover the OSGeo organization and general topics:

| List | Description | Subscribe |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| **OSGeo-Discuss** | General discussion about OSGeo | [Subscribe](https://lists.osgeo.org/mailman/listinfo/discuss) |
| **OSGeo-Board** | Board meeting announcements (read-only) | [Subscribe](https://lists.osgeo.org/mailman/listinfo/board) |
| **OSGeo-Marketing** | Marketing and outreach coordination | [Subscribe](https://lists.osgeo.org/mailman/listinfo/marketing) |

## Mailing List Etiquette

To get the most from mailing lists:

<div class="content">
<ul>
<li><strong>Search first</strong> - Check archives before asking</li>
<li><strong>Be specific</strong> - Include software version, OS, and error messages</li>
<li><strong>Use descriptive subjects</strong> - Help others find your thread</li>
<li><strong>Reply to the list</strong> - Share knowledge with everyone</li>
<li><strong>Be patient</strong> - Everyone is a volunteer</li>
<li><strong>Be respectful</strong> - Follow the <a href="/community/code-of-conduct/">Code of Conduct</a></li>
</ul>
</div>

3. Choose your delivery options
