## How to Apply

### Requirements

To be listed as an OSGeo service provider, organizations should:

- Offer services related to OSGeo software
- Have experience with open source geospatial technology
- Be willing to be publicly listed

### Application Process

1. **Submit your listing** via the OSGeo wiki
2. **Provide details** about your services and expertise
3. **Review** by the OSGeo team
4. **Listing** in the service provider directory

[Submit Your Listing](https://wiki.osgeo.org/wiki/Service_Providers)

---

## What to Include

Your listing should include:

- **Organization name and logo**
- **Contact information**
- **Services offered** (support, training, development, etc.)
- **Geographic coverage**
- **OSGeo projects** you work with
- **Brief description** of your organization
