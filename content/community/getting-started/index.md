<span>Welcome</span>
</div>
<h2 class="section-title">Welcome to OSGeo!</h2>
</div>

Whether you're a GIS professional, developer, researcher, student, or simply curious about open source geospatial technology, there's a place for you in the OSGeo community.

This guide will help you take your first steps into the world of open source geospatial software.

<div class="section-header">
<div class="section-header-badge">
<i class="fa-solid fa-download"></i>
<span>Step 1</span>
</div>
<h2 class="section-title">Try the Software</h2>
</div>

The best way to start is by using OSGeo software. Here are some recommendations based on your needs:

<div class="columns is-multiline mt-4">
<div class="column is-6">
<div class="box">
<h4><i class="fa-solid fa-desktop has-text-success mr-2"></i>Desktop GIS</h4>
<p>For mapping, analysis, and visualization:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/projects/qgis/">QGIS</a> - Most popular, great for beginners</li>
<li><a href="/projects/grass/">GRASS GIS</a> - Powerful for analysis</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div class="column is-6">
<div class="box">
<h4><i class="fa-solid fa-globe has-text-success mr-2"></i>Web Mapping</h4>
<p>For publishing maps online:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/projects/geoserver/">GeoServer</a> - Full-featured map server</li>
<li><a href="/projects/openlayers/">OpenLayers</a> - JavaScript mapping</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div class="column is-6">
<div class="box">
<h4><i class="fa-solid fa-database has-text-success mr-2"></i>Spatial Database</h4>
<p>For storing geospatial data:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/projects/postgis/">PostGIS</a> - PostgreSQL extension</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div class="column is-6">
<div class="box">
<h4><i class="fa-solid fa-compact-disc has-text-success mr-2"></i>Try Everything</h4>
<p>Want to explore without installing?</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/initiatives/osgeolive/">OSGeoLive</a> - 50+ apps ready to run</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
</div>
