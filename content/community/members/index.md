OSGeo welcomes participation from everyone interested in open source geospatial technology. There are several ways to become part of our community:

### Community Participation

Anyone can participate in the OSGeo community by:
- Using OSGeo software
- Contributing to projects
- Joining mailing lists and discussions
- Attending events

Charter Members are individuals who have demonstrated sustained and significant contributions to OSGeo. Charter membership is by nomination.
