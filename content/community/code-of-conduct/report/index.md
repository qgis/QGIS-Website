OSGeo is committed to providing a safe and welcoming environment for all community members. If you have experienced or witnessed behavior that violates our Code of Conduct, please report it.
{{< box-end >}}

---

## How to Report

### Email the Code of Conduct Committee

Send a detailed report to the Code of Conduct Committee:

{{< rich-box-start icon="fa-solid fa-envelope" >}}
### Contact

**Email:** [coc@osgeo.org](mailto:coc@osgeo.org)

All reports are treated confidentially and reviewed by the committee.
{{< rich-box-end >}}

---

## What to Include

When reporting an incident, please include:

- **Date, time, and location** of the incident
- **Description** of what happened
- **Who was involved** (names or descriptions)
- **Any witnesses** who observed the incident
- **Any supporting material** (screenshots, links, etc.)
- **Your contact information** (optional, but helpful for follow-up)

---

## What Happens Next

{{< rich-box-start icon="fa-solid fa-shield-halved" >}}
### Our Process

1. **Acknowledgment** - We will confirm receipt within 48 hours
2. **Review** - The committee will review all information
3. **Investigation** - We may contact you for additional details
4. **Decision** - The committee will determine appropriate action
5. **Communication** - We will inform you of the outcome
{{< rich-box-end >}}

---

## Confidentiality

All reports are treated with strict confidentiality. Information will only be shared with committee members involved in reviewing the report. We will protect reporter identities unless disclosure is required by law or with explicit permission.
