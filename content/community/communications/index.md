OSGeo provides a welcoming inclusive safe space, respecting the voices and opinions of all community members. All OSGeo participants are expected to follow our [Code of Conduct](/community/code-of-conduct/).

<h2 class="section-title">Mailing Lists</h2>
</div>

Mailing lists remain a popular way to communicate with the OSGeo community.

### Discussion List

- **Subscribe:** [lists.osgeo.org/mailman/listinfo/discuss](https://lists.osgeo.org/mailman/listinfo/discuss)
- **Send:** discuss@osgeo.org (subscribers only)
- **Archive:** [Searchable archive](https://lists.osgeo.org/pipermail/discuss/)

### Announcements List

- **Subscribe:** [lists.osgeo.org/mailman/listinfo/announce](https://lists.osgeo.org/mailman/listinfo/announce)
- **Archive:** [Foundation announcements](https://lists.osgeo.org/pipermail/announce/)

OSGeo hosts mailing lists for projects, committees and initiatives.
