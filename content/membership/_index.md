## Charter Membership

Charter Members are individuals who have demonstrated sustained commitment to the OSGeo community. They help guide the foundation's direction and vote in board elections.

{{< columns-start >}}

{{< column-start class="is-6" >}}

### Benefits of Charter Membership

- **Vote** in board elections
- **Nominate** new charter members
- **Participate** in foundation governance
- **Shape** the future of open source geospatial
- **Recognition** on the OSGeo website

{{< column-end >}}

{{< column-start class="is-6" >}}
{{< rich-box-start >}}
### How to Become a Charter Member

Charter membership is **earned through contribution**, not purchased. Paths include:

- Contributing to OSGeo projects
- Organizing events or local chapters
- Advocacy and outreach
- Long-term community participation

Existing Charter Members nominate new members based on their contributions.

[View Charter Members &rarr;](/membership/charter-members/)
{{< rich-box-end >}}
{{< column-end >}}
