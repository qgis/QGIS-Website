<h2 class="section-title">About MetaCRS</h2>
</div>

The MetaCRS initiative gathers together interested parties from across several projections, and coordinate system related technologies. It was felt that there was sufficient common institutional infrastructure that a combined effort would be valuable.

The initiative provides a space for sharing:
- Coordinate system dictionaries
- Test suites
- Mathematical formulations
