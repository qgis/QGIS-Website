<h2 class="section-title">Our Partners</h2>
</div>

OSGeo collaborates with a variety of organizations to advance open source geospatial technology:

{{< columns-start >}}

{{< column-start class="is-6" >}}

{{< rich-box-start icon="fa-solid fa-globe" >}}
### Standards Organizations

OSGeo works closely with standards bodies to ensure interoperability:

- **Open Geospatial Consortium (OGC)**
- **ISO Technical Committee 211**
- **INSPIRE**
{{< rich-box-end >}}

{{< rich-box-start icon="fa-solid fa-graduation-cap" >}}
### Academic Partners

Through Geo for All, we partner with educational institutions worldwide:

- **International Cartographic Association (ICA)**
- **International Society for Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing (ISPRS)**
- **Association of Geographic Information Laboratories in Europe (AGILE)**
- **University Consortium for Geographic Information Science (UCGIS)**
{{< rich-box-end >}}

{{< column-end >}}

{{< column-start class="is-6" >}}

{{< rich-box-start icon="fa-solid fa-earth-americas" >}}
### International Organizations

OSGeo engages with international bodies on geospatial matters:

- **United Nations (UN-GGIM)**
- **Group on Earth Observations (GEO)**
- **World Bank**
{{< rich-box-end >}}

{{< rich-box-start icon="fa-solid fa-building" >}}
### Industry Partners

We work with industry to promote open source adoption:

- Service providers and consultancies
- Software vendors implementing open standards
- Data providers releasing open data
{{< rich-box-end >}}

{{< column-end >}}
