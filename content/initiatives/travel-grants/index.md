## About Travel Grants

OSGeo is committed to ensuring that financial constraints don't prevent community members from attending FOSS4G conferences. Our Travel Grant Program (TGP) provides financial assistance to help cover travel, accommodation, and registration costs.

Since its inception, the program has helped hundreds of community members from around the world attend FOSS4G, enriching the conference with diverse perspectives and experiences.

## What's Covered?

Grants can cover:

- **Conference registration** - Full or partial registration fees
- **Travel costs** - Economy airfare or ground transportation
- **Accommodation** - Shared or individual accommodation during the conference
- **Daily allowance** - Per diem for meals and incidentals

Grant amounts vary based on available funding and individual circumstances. Partial grants may be awarded to support more applicants.

## Selection Criteria

Applications are evaluated based on:

### Contribution to Community
- Involvement in OSGeo projects
- Open source advocacy work
- Community organizing and outreach
- Documentation and translation

### Presentation Quality
- Accepted talks or workshops
- Relevance to FOSS4G themes
- Potential to share knowledge

### Need and Impact
- Financial need
- Geographic underrepresentation
- Career impact of attendance
- Commitment to share knowledge after attending

### Diversity Factors
- Gender diversity
- Geographic diversity
- Career stage
- First-time attendees

### How to Apply

1. **Submit your application** through the official form when applications open
2. **Provide documentation** of your involvement in the community
3. **Explain your situation** and how attending would benefit you and the community
4. **Indicate your needs** - travel, accommodation, registration
5. **Commit to participate** - attend sessions, network, share knowledge

### Application Tips

- **Apply early** - Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis
- **Be specific** - Clearly explain your contributions and needs
- **Show commitment** - Demonstrate how you'll give back to the community
- **Budget realistically** - Provide accurate cost estimates
