<h2 class="section-title">Trademark Guidelines</h2>
</div>

The Open Source Geospatial Foundation emphasizes building market presence through controlled brand association. The organization seeks to balance legal oversight of its symbols with openness, following similar approaches used by comparable organizations like Apache and Mozilla.

---

<div class="section-header">
<div class="section-header-badge">
<i class="fa-solid fa-circle-info"></i>
<span>Overview</span>
</div>
<h2 class="section-title">Introduction</h2>
</div>

This policy governs use of OSGeo trademarks, defined as the name OSGeo in the variations listed below, OSGeo's logos and the names of its various products. The foundation aims to maintain trademarks as reliable indicators of quality and security while allowing community discussion and accurate affiliation descriptions.

---

<div class="section-header">
<div class="section-header-badge">
<i class="fa-solid fa-scale-balanced"></i>
<span>Principle</span>
</div>
<h2 class="section-title">Core Principle</h2>
</div>

The overarching requirement states that trademark usage must be **non-confusing and non-disparaging**, meaning users must clearly identify sources and cannot use marks to damage the foundation's reputation.

---

<div class="section-header">
<div class="section-header-badge">
<i class="fa-solid fa-registered"></i>
<span>Marks</span>
</div>
<h2 class="section-title">OSGeo Trademarks</h2>
</div>

The following are protected OSGeo trademarks:

{{< columns-start >}}

{{< column-start class="is-6" >}}

- The Open Source Geospatial Foundation
- OSGeo
- OSGeo.org

{{< column-end >}}

{{< column-start class="is-6" >}}

<div class="section-header">
<div class="section-header-badge">
<i class="fa-solid fa-list-check"></i>
<span>Guidelines</span>
</div>
<h2 class="section-title">Usage Guidelines</h2>
</div>

{{< rich-box-start icon="fa-solid fa-print" >}}
### Printed Materials

Marketing and publicity materials may use trademarks if they don't create confusion about product origin or affiliation. You may not misrepresent unaffiliated software as OSGeo products or claim fundraising for local chapters as Foundation fundraising.
{{< rich-box-end >}}

{{< rich-box-start icon="fa-solid fa-link" >}}
### Linking

Website links to OSGeo resources are permitted as long as they don't confuse visitors or violate overall guidelines.
{{< rich-box-end >}}

{{< rich-box-start icon="fa-solid fa-globe" >}}
### Domain Names

Written OSGeo permission is required for incorporating trademarks into domain names. Domain similarity naturally suggests organizational connection, creating confusion risks.
{{< rich-box-end >}}

{{< rich-box-start icon="fa-solid fa-briefcase" >}}
### Services

Describing services related to OSGeo software is allowed with clear attribution statements. Marketing language should explicitly identify your organization separately from OSGeo. You may not imply direct Foundation involvement.
{{< rich-box-end >}}

{{< rich-box-start icon="fa-solid fa-palette" >}}
### Logos

Modified versions of OSGeo logos are prohibited except for local chapters. Commercial merchandise cannot display OSGeo branding without authorization. The Foundation provides approved logo variations through branding materials.

[View Branding Materials &rarr;](/about/branding/)
{{< rich-box-end >}}
