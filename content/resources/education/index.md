<h2 class="section-title">Geo for All</h2>
</div>

OSGeo's flagship educational initiative, Geo for All, promotes open geospatial education through a global network of university labs.

### Lab Network

Over 100 Geo for All labs at universities worldwide provide:
- Open source geospatial curriculum
- Research opportunities
- Student projects and internships
- Community collaboration

[Learn More About Geo for All](/initiatives/geo-for-all/){.button .is-osgeo-secondary}

<h2 class="section-title">Learning Resources</h2>

### Official Documentation

Each OSGeo project maintains comprehensive documentation:

- **QGIS** - [docs.qgis.org](https://docs.qgis.org)
- **GRASS GIS** - [grass.osgeo.org/documentation](https://grass.osgeo.org/documentation/)
- **PostGIS** - [postgis.net/documentation](https://postgis.net/documentation/)
- **GeoServer** - [docs.geoserver.org](https://docs.geoserver.org)

### OSGeoLive

The best way to learn multiple OSGeo applications at once. Includes quick start guides for every application.

[Get OSGeoLive](/initiatives/osgeolive/)

### Online Courses

Many universities and organizations offer courses using OSGeo software:

- University GIS courses
- Professional training programs
- Online platforms (Coursera, Udemy, etc.)
- FOSS4G workshops
